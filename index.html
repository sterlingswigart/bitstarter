Oh shit! The yucca again? I don't know how it happens, but no matter how hearty they may be, my wife and I manage to kill every house plant that we buy. Never again! With the new Rememb-o-tron visual alarm, receive non-intrusive but easily actionable feedback on any set schedule, in any place you want!

Stick one in an inconspicuous wall next to your plant, and have it slowly pulse green when the plant needs watering. Or stick a small one next to your fuel guage to remind you to change the oil every three months - when you're actually in a position to do it! Set up and configure each unit hassle-free from your smartphone, either via Wifi for those in the house or via Bluetooth for those remote units in your car or yard.

Rememb-o-trons come in a variety of sizes for placement in any situation. These waterproof units can be stuck to just about any surface, so go ahead and put these around your house, in your car, or out in your yard. Use your handy iPhone or Android to easily configure:

  • Alarm schedule
  • Color and pulse of the visual alarm
  • Volume and sound if you would like an auditory alarm

FAQ

How much will each unit cost?
We're aiming to price each unit between 5 and 15 dollars, or 30 for a set of 5.
Why shouldn't I just remind myself to do things with my phone?
Receiving reminders isn't any good if you're not where the chore is. If I'm at work, I can't water my plant at home! By placing rememb-o-trons at the actual site of what needs to be done, I can be reminded to do it at a time that is convenient for me.
